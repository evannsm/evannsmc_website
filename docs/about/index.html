Community

BORI at Georgia Tech

Our latest tradition is to sarcastically give credit to the organization whenever literally anything happens to us.... One of our members lost 45 pounds all thanks to BORI

I co-founded BORI (Boricuas Organized for Impact) to make sure Puerto Rican students at Georgia Tech had a space that felt familiar, welcoming, and (hopefully) professionally useful.

  • We build events around culture, food, and fun ...professional development
  • We host events focused on making new students feel comfortable in Atlanta and at Georgia Tech.
  • BORI also helped me keep up with the latest slang ever since I left the island, and to no longer feel so distant from the culture I grew up with.
