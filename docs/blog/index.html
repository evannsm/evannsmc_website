Newton-Raphson Flow: From Iteration to Continuous Control

2025-05-22 published

The Newton-Raphson flow tracking controller — its derivation from the iterative root-finding method, its adaptation for dynamical systems via output prediction, and why it stays light enough to deploy onboard a real quadrotor.

Research Note 2025-05-22

Newton-Raphson Flow:
From Iteration to Continuous Control

E. Morales-Cuadrado · 2025

Abstract

We reframe reference tracking as a continuous-time Newton-Raphson root-finding problem. The iterative update law is adapted for dynamical systems via output prediction, yielding a controller that is computationally lightweight, easy to retune, and demonstrably deployable onboard a quadrotor UAV.
